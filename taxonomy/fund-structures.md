# Fund Structures

This document outlines common fund structures used in private markets.

## Closed-End Funds
Funds with a fixed life and capital commitment period, commonly used in private equity, private credit, and real assets.

## Evergreen Funds
Open-ended structures allowing ongoing subscriptions and redemptions, subject to liquidity terms.

## Continuation Vehicles
GP-led secondary structures used to extend ownership of existing assets beyond the original fund term.
