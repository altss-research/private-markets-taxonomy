# Allocator Types

This document outlines common allocator categories observed in private markets.

## Family Offices
Private investment organizations managing capital on behalf of high-net-worth families. Allocation behavior varies by governance structure, generation, and liquidity requirements.

## Pension Funds
Institutional investors managing retirement assets with long-duration liabilities and regulated allocation constraints.

## Foundations & Endowments
Mission-driven allocators with perpetual capital horizons and recurring spending requirements.

## Sovereign Wealth Funds
State-owned investment entities managing excess reserves or strategic national capital.
